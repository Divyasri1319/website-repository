The Problem

Children under 3 are spending more time on mobile devices, often unsupervised. This can affect their development, sleep, and mental health. But how can we stop it—automatically?

How SafeScreen AI Works

  • 🔍 Uses your phone’s front camera to detect who’s using the device
  • 👦 Locks the screen if a child is detected
  • 📲 Sends an instant alert to the parent’s phone
  • 🛡 Works with any app, in the background
